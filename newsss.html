Various Groups File Impeachment Complaint Against VP Duterte

Members of various groups filed an impeachment complaint against Vice President Sara Duterte before the House of Representatives Monday afternoon.

Acting as complainants are members of civil society organizations, religious leaders, sectoral representatives, and families of those who died during the war on drugs of Former President Rodrigo Duterte.

Akbayan Partylist Representative Perci Cendana endorsed the impeachment complaint against the vice president.
